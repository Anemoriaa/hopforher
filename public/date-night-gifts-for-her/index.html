FAQ

Quick answers

What makes a good date-night gift instead of a regular gift?

The gift should sharpen the reveal, the getting-ready moment, or the mood of the night itself instead of feeling like a random everyday object.

What is the safest date-night gift on this page?

The Marc Jacobs fragrance and Sol de Janeiro set are the cleanest first answers because they feel date-facing, present-ready, and do not depend on getting her size exactly right.

When should I choose anniversary gifts instead of date-night gifts?

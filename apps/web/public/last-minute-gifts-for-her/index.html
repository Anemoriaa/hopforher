Buyer read

What usually works here

Clarity beats originality

When you are short on time, the best gift is the one that reads correctly and cleanly on first impression.

Fast-recognition products help

Products with obvious use cases or familiar brand energy make last-minute shopping look more intentional.

Avoid explanation-heavy buys

If you need to narrate why the gift is good, it is weaker than a simpler product that sells itself immediately.

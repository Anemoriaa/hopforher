FAQ

Quick answers

Do anniversary gifts need to be sentimental?

Not necessarily, but they should feel intentional. Experience-adjacent and premium home gifts often work better than purely practical ones.

Is a shared-experience gift better than a basic product?

Often yes. The projector, digital frame, and similar picks feel stronger because they create a moment instead of just adding another object.

Should I optimize for romance or usefulness on an anniversary?

Romance or emotional signal should lead. Usefulness helps, but it should not be the only reason the gift is there.

Product Description

Creativity is in the AiR with the INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Smartphone Printer The INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Smartphone Printer connects to your Smartphone wirelessly in a simple, yet super fun way! Connect to your Smartphone via the free downloadable INSTAX MINI LINK App and watch creativity flow with the all-new INSTAXAiR feature. Add bubbles, petals, neon, spray paint or even glitter to your Smartphone photo by drawing in the air with the INSTAX MINI LINK 2 device, all before printing out onto INSTAX MINI instant film. You can even record a video of the drawing and share the moment as a printable QR code, ready to scan with a Smartphone. There are so many ways to add your personality to your photos using the INSTAXAiR motion control, the fast-paced Match Test feature including the “Go with your instinct” Mode, the Print from Video feature, plus so much more! This striking printer packs a whole lot of creativity! Bundle includes 1 - 1-pk Instax Mini film.

From the manufacturer

The NEW INSTAX MINI LINK 2

Creativity is in the AiR

The INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Smartphone Printer inspires creativity and priceless interactions with your friends and family. Connect the printer to your Smartphone via the free, downloadable INSTAX MINI LINK App and watch creativity flow with the all-new INSTAXAiR feature and the much-loved Match Test App feature. Get ready to print and give more with the INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Smartphone Printer!

INSTAXAiR

Blast off

Use Bluetooth connectivity to pair your Smartphone, download the free INSTAX MINI LINK App and watch creativity flow with the all-new INSTAXAiR feature. Add bubbles, petals, neon, spray paint or even glitter to your Smartphone image by drawing in the air with the INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Printer, all before printing out onto INSTAX MINI instant film.

SIMPLE PRINT

Swipe ‘n’ Print

Getting your images into the real world is both easy and quick using the free, downloadable INSTAX MINI LINK App. Connect your Smartphone wirelessly to the INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Smartphone Printer, select the photo on your Smartphone you want to print, add text, resize, apply a filter, and play with the contrasts before swiping up to print.

VIDEO PRINT

Poetry in motion

Not all experiences are captured motionless - who doesn’t love a memory from a favorite video? With the free, downloadable INSTAX MINI LINK App, you can scroll through your favorite videos, select that magical moment, and print an INSTAX MINI image of it on your INSTAX MINI LINK 2 Smartphone printer!

MATCH TEST

Great minds…

…think alike? Test your compatibility with you and your friends by using the “Go with your instinct” Mode in the Match Test App feature and wait for the printer to print your compatibility score (up to 100) on your photo. If you’re more of a quiz sort of gang, answer the questions and print out the results on a picture of you and your BFF.
